# Bounty #62 – Legal Bounty

The Polkadot Legal Bounty provides treasury-funded legal research and resources for the Polkadot DAO ecosystem, including academic research on blockchain governance, PCF structure, and legal frameworks.

## Primary Documentation

- Official website: https://www.polkadot.law/legalbounty
- Original proposal: https://polkadot.subsquare.io/referenda/1359
- Curator approval: https://polkadot.subsquare.io/referenda/1407

## Current Status

⚠️ **Note:** There appears to be a recent proposal for "Closing Bounty 62. Polkadot Legal Bounty", suggesting the bounty may be in the process of being wound down.

## Services Provided

The Legal Bounty offers comprehensive legal support for the Polkadot ecosystem:

- Legal research and analysis
- Academic research on blockchain governance
- PCF (Polkadot Community Foundation) structure guidance
- Legal frameworks for DAO operations
- Professional legal consultation for ecosystem participants

## How to Contact

People can contact the bounty curators through:
- **Discord**: Available via polkadot.law website
- **Website**: https://www.polkadot.law/legalbounty

## Purpose

The Legal Bounty serves to:
- Provide accessible legal resources to the Polkadot community
- Support informed decision-making on governance matters
- Offer professional legal guidance for ecosystem projects
- Research and document legal frameworks applicable to DAOs and blockchain governance
