HOW IT WORKS

Submit a Proposal

Event organizers must submit their funding proposal at least one month before the event date via the form. Proposals should include clear objectives, budget, success metrics and event details.

ABOUT

ABOUT

The Polkadot Events Bounty is its own entity and treasury pot elected by the token holders and community via OpenGov. This bounty has been established for the purpose of: 

  1. Positioning Polkadot, valuable ecosystem teams, individuals and agents at the most impactful events around the globe 

  2. Fostering community growth across multiple regions around the globe 

  3. Nurturing events teams to maintain their various regions 

  4. Creating a fast, transparent and viable system for events oversight and execution
