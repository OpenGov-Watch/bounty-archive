You can apply to receive funds to cover your Polkadot meetup up to 1000 USD in DOT (plus up to 300 USD as a reward). Anyone active in the Polkadot community can apply AFTER the meetup happens if they fulfill all the conditions. Please fill out the form only if your event has already happened. If you are curious about whether your proposal has a chance to pass in the future, feel free to ask and connect with us in our Telegram group.

Before you organize a meetup, take time to read all the information on this page in all subcategories. There are rules you must follow; not following them might lead to the rejection of your proposal.
